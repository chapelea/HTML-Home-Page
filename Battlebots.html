Interests - BattleBots

Previous Next

BattleBots is a game show in which teams fight using robots of their design. Two bots are placed in a plexiglass arena, and are given a countdown. The players must then render their opponents bot unusable. There is a lot of destruction, and a lot of crushed dreams.

When I was growing up, my dad and I didn't get along very well. There were only a select few things that we did together. One of those things was watching BattleBots. He would bring home fast food, and we'd eat in the living room and watch. I always told him that one day I'd be up there, and that I'd have a bot of my own. To this day, building a BattleBot is a dream of mine.
